Here's the plan:

Our great copy is used to give an explanation of why visitors need our product or service.

Oftentimes we need a second paragraph to reinforce the point. We can use this paragraph to show that our product/service solves a problem - even if the visitor hasn't realised they need this problem solved.

And then a third one to seal the deal. Here we let visitors know that without our product/service they are losing money/time/clients. Who wants that as a goal?

Added bonus note: keep the landing page simple, with as few distractions as possible - we have removed the menu, sidebars, social media - anything that distracts your visitor from completing the call to action.
